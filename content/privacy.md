**Last Updated:** December 20, 2024

## Overview

Waco Data Center Community Action ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Email Newsletter Signup
When you subscribe to our email list, we collect:
- Your name (optional)
- Your email address
- Your ZIP code (optional)

### Website Analytics
We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This may include:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- General location (city/state level)
- Device type (mobile/desktop)

**We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels.**

## How We Use Your Information

- **Email List:** To send updates about community meetings, new articles, and action items
- **Analytics:** To improve our website and understand what content is most useful

## Data Storage & Security

- Email signups are processed and stored by Netlify (our hosting provider)
- All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS
- We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Unsubscribe** at any time (link in every email)
- **Request deletion** of your data by emailing contact@wacodatacenter.com
- **Request a copy** of your data

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Netlify** for hosting and form processing ([Netlify Privacy Policy](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/))
- **Change.org** for our petition (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Social Media** (Facebook, Instagram) when you click our links (subject to their policies)

## Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will note the date at the top. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.
