KEY DIFFERENCES - COUNTY VS. CITY LETTERS:

COUNTY COMMISSIONERS FOCUS ON:
* County road maintenance and improvements
* Rural infrastructure impacts
* County tax revenue and expenses
* Emergency services (Sheriff, EMS, Fire)
* Groundwater and rural water systems
* Agricultural land use and property rights
* Precinct-specific concerns

MENTION THESE COUNTY RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Farm-to-Market (FM) road maintenance
- County water districts and groundwater
- Rural emergency response times
- County drainage and flood control
- Impact on county budget and tax rates
- Coordination between precincts

TIPS FOR COUNTY-LEVEL ADVOCACY:

* Know your precinct number (affects which commissioner represents you)
* Emphasize county-specific impacts (roads, rural services, taxes)
* Cite comparable rural/county case studies
* Mention agricultural or property rights concerns if applicable
* Request coordination between county and city officials
* Ask about county permits, plat approvals, or road agreements
* Reference county comprehensive plans or development codes
