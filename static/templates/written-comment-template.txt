SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS:

DEADLINES:
- Submit by 5:00 PM the day before the meeting (check local rules)
- Earlier is better - gives officials time to review

HOW TO SUBMIT:
[ ] Email to City Secretary or County Clerk (get email from website)
[ ] Hand-deliver to office during business hours
[ ] Fax (if still accepted - call to confirm)
[ ] U.S. Mail (must arrive before meeting)

FOLLOW UP:
* Call to confirm receipt
* Ask if comment will be included in meeting packet
* Request copy of meeting minutes after the meeting
* Your comment should appear in official records

===================================================================

TIPS FOR EFFECTIVE WRITTEN COMMENTS:

* Keep it to one page maximum
* Include full contact information
* Reference specific agenda item number
* Be clear whether you support or oppose
* Use facts and cite sources
* Be respectful and professional
* Make specific, actionable requests
* Proofread before submitting

- Don't submit anonymous comments (they may be ignored)
- Don't wait until last minute (submission deadlines vary)
- Don't make personal attacks
- Don't assume your comment will be read aloud (it may only be recorded)
